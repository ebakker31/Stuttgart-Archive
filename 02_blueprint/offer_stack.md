## Tiers

## Refund policy (draft direction)

14-day no-questions refund (digital goods norm; aligns with EU consumer expectations even where
digital-content exemptions could apply). Low refund risk: demo dataset + honest copy reduce
mismatch buys. Full draft in Phase 6.

## Upsell / cross-sell map

Buy FitLedger → (later) Pro upgrade → (optional) future template/bonus packs. No email-spam
funnel; one launch list with product updates only.
