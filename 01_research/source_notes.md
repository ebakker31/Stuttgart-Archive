## AI governance / EU AI Act (Idea #1)

- EU AI Act penalties for non-compliance begin **August 2, 2026**; SMB-focused guidance exists from the Act's own outreach site — [Small Businesses' Guide to the AI Act](https://artificialintelligenceact.eu/small-businesses-guide-to-the-ai-act/), [EU AI Act for SMEs: 7-Step Compliance Plan Before Aug 2026 (Delbion)](https://www.delbion.com/en/insights/eu-ai-act-sme-compliance-guide/)
- SMB compliance is documented as disproportionately hard; calls for simplified templates — [AI Policy Bulletin: It's too hard for SMBs to comply with the EU AI Act](https://www.aipolicybulletin.org/articles/its-too-hard-for-small-and-medium-sized-businesses-to-comply-with-eu-ai-act-heres-what-to-do)
- SMB cost estimates around €12,000 per high-risk system; dedicated SMB compliance SaaS emerging — [AIComply](https://www.getaicomply.com/), [Legal Nodes: EU AI Act 2026 updates](https://www.legalnodes.com/article/eu-ai-act-2026-updates-compliance-requirements-and-business-risks)
- 68% of U.S. small businesses now use AI regularly (QuickBooks survey, up from 48% mid-2024) — [ColorWhistle AI statistics for small business, 2026](https://colorwhistle.com/artificial-intelligence-statistics-for-small-business/)
- Federal SMB AI training rolling out ("AI for Main Street Act of 2026") signals mainstream SMB AI-adoption demand — [AdVenture Media on the federal AI curriculum](https://adventuremedia.ai/blog/ai-training-for-small-businesses-in-2026-what-the-federal-curriculum-actually-teaches-you)
- Prompt libraries treated as essential AI-rollout infrastructure — [Gethyn Ellis: Why your Copilot programme needs a prompt library](https://www.gethynellis.com/2026/05/why-your-copilot-programme-needs-a-prompt-library-not-another-pilot/)
