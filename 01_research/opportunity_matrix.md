## Scoring rationale highlights

- **#1 AI Governance Toolkit:** Strongest trend support of any idea — EU AI Act penalties begin
  **Aug 2, 2026**, SMB compliance is documented as confusingly hard, 68% of US small businesses
  now use AI regularly, and the existing market splits into *free single templates* (SHRM, AIHR,
  HiBob — commoditized) and *$99–300/mo SaaS* — leaving a gap for a complete one-time-purchase
  kit. Legal risk is real (must never present as legal advice) but manageable with framing,
  disclaimers, and "review with counsel" instructions. Trend rather than evergreen, but
  regulation-driven products reward ongoing updates (a strength per the project brief).
- **#2 Collector Car Toolkit:** Lowest competition of the top ideas (existing products are
  single bill-of-sale PDFs or free blog checklists; no complete documentation/sale-prep system
  found). High-ticket asset = high willingness to pay at the moment of sale. Unique synergy:
  this repo already contains Stuttgart Archive, a live product in this exact niche — the toolkit
  doubles as a lead-gen/upsell asset. Avoids legal-document drafting (points to state DMV forms
  instead of drafting bills of sale) to keep risk down.
- **#3 Client Onboarding Kit:** Proven, validated price band ($20–75) and an active buyer
  market on Gumroad/Etsy, very fast to build, near-zero legal risk — but crowded, so it wins on
  execution/positioning rather than a structural gap.
- **#10 Hiring Compliance** scored down for legal risk (employment law varies by state; high
  liability if wrong). **#12 Mini-course** scored down on documented course fatigue (templates
  outsell courses ~3:1). **#13 Social templates** fails the brief's "B2B/high-pain over generic
  consumer" rule.

## Recommended top 3

1. **Option 1 — SMB AI Governance & Adoption Toolkit** (score 78). Best combination of urgency,
   B2B willingness to pay, and a real gap between free fragments and expensive SaaS. Suggested
   price band $49–149.
2. **Option 2 — Collector Car Sale-Prep & Documentation Toolkit** (score 77). Lowest competition,
   strongest differentiation, and strategic fit with the Stuttgart Archive platform already in
   this repo. Suggested price band $29–79.
3. **Option 3 — Client Onboarding & Offboarding Kit Pro** (score 72). Safest, fastest, most
   proven; lowest ceiling. Suggested price band $29–59.
